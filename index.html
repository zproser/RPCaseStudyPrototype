/ Securitas

Building the future of human-first security

We partnered with Securitas to turn a complex, behind-the-scenes operation into something clearer, faster, and a little more human—without lowering the guardrails.

/ Overview

A business transformation plan and digital tools that reinvent how employees and clients interact.

To understand the security space, we ran dozens of in-person and remote interviews—then refined what we learned into personas, user journeys, and digital ecosystem recommendations so the work ahead had a shared foundation.

/ Objective

Reinvent how building security staff and clients interact, and track what every change means for roles and the business.

/ 001

Anchor the program in a clear goal: better interactions between people on the ground and the clients they serve—without losing sight of structure, accountability, or outcomes.

/ 002

Ground decisions in depth: 900+ hours of research, 45 interviews, four system evaluations, and 1000+ findings distilled into opportunity areas, insights, and moments that matter.

/ 003

Align leadership and teams through discovery sessions with top stakeholders—so priorities, constraints, and sequencing stayed connected to the real operation.

/ 004

Set up delivery for what comes next: a shared view of the digital ecosystem, a practical roadmap, and communications that carry the change through rollout—not just the kickoff.

/ Solution

Dashboards, operating model, roadmap, and a plan to bring everyone along.

The output wasn’t a single screen—it was a connected set of decisions: new client and manager experiences, an operating model teams could run, a digital roadmap tied to evidence, and a rollout communications plan so change didn’t stop at the workshop wall.

Client & manager dashboards

New dashboards for clients and managers—built on customer and employee research, discovery with stakeholders, and a rollout story that matched how Securitas actually works day to day.

/ Impact

900+ hours of research—one plan for what to build next.
